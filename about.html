Our Vision

The Pursuit of Truth

Equipping Leaders for Positive Change.

Mission: Send It exists to Seek, Engage, Nourish, and Direct In Truth by equipping leaders, strengthening resilience, and meeting people where they are for positive change and productivity.

Vision: To develop individuals and communities with spiritual resilience who are purpose-driven and equipped to lead with truth and integrity.

Our Core Values

The foundational pillars that guide everything we do as an organization.

Moral Leadership

Leading by example with unwavering ethics and sound moral principles rooted in biblical values.

Spiritual Resilience

Building the internal fortitude to navigate complex problems and overcome adversity with faith.

Community Engagement

Meeting people exactly where they are to foster collaboration and collective well-being.

Human Dignity

Treating every individual with the highest level of respect, acknowledging their inherent worth.

Personal Accountability

Taking ownership of our actions, decisions, and impact on the world around us.
